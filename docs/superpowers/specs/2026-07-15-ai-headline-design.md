# AI-First Headline Redesign

## Goal
Make the "Ask the AI about me" feature more prominent in the portfolio's Hero section without cluttering the UI.

## Chosen Approach
We will change the main hero headline to explicitly introduce the AI chat feature, shifting the narrative to be AI-first. 

## Copy Updates
**New Tagline (Headline):**
"I engineer intelligent enterprise solutions. Ask my AI anything about my experience."

The subtext (paragraph) and the "Ask the AI about me" button will remain structurally the same, but the new headline will immediately draw attention to the AI capability.

## Implementation Details
- Update `profile.tagline` in `src/lib/profile.ts` to the new copy.
- No structural or component changes are required in `Hero.tsx`.
